Welcome Group 7's Project


Austin, TX House Listings

With the boom in the Austin housing market, we wanted to showcase how the market has changed over time and what factors may impact housing purchases. We looked at several attributes in the dataset to gain insight such as school rating, homeowner associations, home type and amenities.

Motivation

Given the popularity of the greater Austin Texas area, it would be interesting to have an understanding of the market through the lens of a first time home buyer.

Looking Forward

As a home buyer there are many things to consider when investing in a home. While this data doesn't predict future trends, it may shed light on the popularity of certain house types, locations, or related community factors.
